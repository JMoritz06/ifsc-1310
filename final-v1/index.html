Overview

Descend into the depths of an alien underwater world filled with wonder and peril. Craft equipment, pilot submarines and out-smart wildlife to explore lush coral reefs, volcanoes, cave systems, and more - all while trying to survive. In this survival game you need to prioritize your needs like food, hydration, power, and even oxygen. But there are things on this planet that are not meant to be uncovered.
